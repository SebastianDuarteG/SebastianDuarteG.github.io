Chipotle Mexican Grill

Founded in 1993, Chipotle has been providing its customers with fast and affordable tacos, burritos, and bowls. For a Spartan that wants a fast and filling experience, this is an amazing choice. Located at 435 Tate Street, it is within walking distance of the university. Open from 10:45 am to 11 pm every day.

Chick-Fil-A

Founded in 1946, Chick-Fil-A has been providing its customers with fast and tasty chicken sandwiches, nuggets, and chicken salads. Spartans looking for a tasty, protein-full meal, can find a good option here. Located at the first floor of the Elliot Center. Open from 7:30 am to 10 pm during the week, it is a perfect in-campus option.
